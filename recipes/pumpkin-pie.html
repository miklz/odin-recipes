Pumpkin Pie

Description

Pumpkin pie is an all-time holiday dessert classic. The contrast of spiced pumpkin against cold, tangy cream cheese is a gift I look forward to every fall.

This recipe swaps out cream cheese for swirl of luscious, smooth, mascarpone.

Ingredients

  • 1 (9 inch) unbaked pie crust
  • 1 (15 ounce) can pumpkin puree
  • 1 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon pumpkin pie spice

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C).
  2. Fit pie crust into a 9-inch pie dish and place it on a baking sheet.
  3. Combine pumpkin puree, condensed milk, eggs, and pumpkin pie spice in a large bowl. Mix until combined and set aside.
  4. Make the cheesecake swirl: In a medium, microwave-safe bowl stir together mascarpone, milk, maple syrup, and flour.
  5. Microwave on high power until the cheese is completely melted, 20 to 30 seconds. Stir the mixture until completely smooth. (This will make swirling easier.)
  6. Add about two-thirds of the cheese mixture into the pumpkin filling and stir to combine.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 15 minutes.
  8. Remove from the oven, set on a wire rack, and cool completely before serving.
