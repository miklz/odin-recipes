Maple Salmon

Description

This maple glazed salmon is delicious and very easy to prepare. This restaurant-worthy maple salmon recipe is as good as it gets! Plus, it's easy to make with simple ingredients you probably already have on hand.

Ingredients

  • Maple syrup
  • Soy sauce
  • Garlic
  • Seasonings
  • Salmon

Steps

  1. Make the marinade and cut the salmon into fillets.
  2. Marinate in the fridge for at least 30 minutes.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until the fish flakes easily.
